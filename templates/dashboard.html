Data Constraints and Analytical Methods

Known Data Constraints:

  • Emissions: National aggregates only; county-level disaggregation unavailable in source data
  • Demographics: Single census year (2022); temporal gaps filled via forward propagation
  • Water Quality: Limited to designated bathing sites; not representative of all water bodies
  • Geographic Scope: Partial county coverage due to data availability constraints

Data Quality Indicators:

  • Primary sources: Central Statistics Office Ireland
  • Temporal coverage: 2021-2024 (water quality)
  • Emission inventory: Complete national time series
  • Spatial resolution: County administrative boundaries

Analytical Methodology:

  • Pollution Index: Composite metric derived from multi-pollutant aggregation
  • County Allocation: Population-weighted distribution of national emissions
  • Water Quality: Site-level measurements averaged to county scale
  • Statistical Tests: Pearson correlation with significance testing

Visualization Methods:

  • Time series: Temporal trend identification
  • Scatter plots: Bivariate relationship analysis
  • Heatmaps: Correlation matrix representation
  • Bar charts: Cross-sectional comparisons
  • Box plots: Distribution characterization
  • Tables: Detailed numerical summaries
